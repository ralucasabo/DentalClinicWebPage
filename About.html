Our proud team

Dent Clinic is a dream come true; a concept dental clinic, a place where patients come warmly and feel at home. Dent is a welcoming and modern digital space, humanized by a tailor-made star team. Dent is about us, the people behind the concept. We are guided by two ideas that best foreshadow the identity of our dental clinic: “Go Digital” and “One Session Dentistry”. The integrated digital dental laboratory has state-of-the-art equipment. 3D imaging equipment is the best of all available on the market.

We love the digital technologies and always try to integrate the most advanced tech, from intra-oral scanners to 3D dental printers. All this shortens working times and brings ultra-precision in our daily work. Thus, you will benefit from better performing and completed dental work much faster compared to the work done by classical dental techniques. So we are a big family! We spend a lot of time in the clinic. We want to form a professional, welcoming and friendly team, so that you, our patients, can return to treatments with a Dent smile on your face. We collaborate interdisciplinary in most cases, in order to always identify the best treatment possibilities for you.
